Steps to make a sandwich

  1. Place two slices of Oro Wheat 12 grain bread on a plate.
  2. Open a jar of Jif crunchy peanut butter.
  3. Remove 2 ounces of peanut butter from the Jif jar and place on the left slice of bread located on the plate. Spread peanut butter evenly across the slice of bread with a butter knife.
  4. Open jar of Knotts Berry Farm raspberry jelly.
  5. Remove 2 ounces of raspberry jelly from the Knotts Berry Farm jar and place on the right slice of bread located on the plate. Spread raspberry jelly evenly across the slice of bread with a butter knife.
  6. Place the left slice of bread on top of the right slice of bread, located on the plate.
  7. Using a knife, cut the combined left and right slices of bread into two even halves.
